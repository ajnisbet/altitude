High-resolution elevation API with worldwide coverage.

TopoData serves digital elevation model (DEM) data over a RESTful API.

The service can be used to give a user's current altitude, computing steepness, and generating elevation profiles for routes.

Global coverage

Our 5TB dataset covers the whole globe, including arctic and antarctic regions.

High resolution

100m base resolution, with high resolution LIDAR data up to 1m where available.

Maintenance free

We take care of storing, processing, and updating data so you don't have to.

Google compatible

We have endpoints supporting the Google Elevation API syntax, for easy migration.

Bathymetry

Underwater depth can be given for oceans and lakes.

Support

We're always available to help. Email support@topodata.io any time.
